Second Place at BINOCUP 2023

At BINOCUP 2023, I secured third place by presenting a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of an AI-powered medical assistance software. This innovative solution was designed to support radiologists in analyzing medical images, including MRIs, X-rays, and other diagnostic scans. The system achieved high accuracy in detecting critical conditions such as breast cancer and brain tumors, enabling faster diagnoses and improving patient outcomes through early detection of pathological anomalies.

  • Developed an AI-powered tool for radiologists to enhance diagnostic accuracy.
  • Focused on detecting breast cancer and brain tumors with high precision.
  • Presented a scalable MVP with potential for clinical integration.
