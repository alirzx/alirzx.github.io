About Me

I am an AI/ML and Computer Vision Engineer with a strong passion for leveraging cutting-edge technologies to solve complex real-world problems. With a solid foundation in deep learning, machine learning, and computer vision, I specialize in designing, developing, and deploying intelligent systems that drive innovation and efficiency.

My expertise spans a wide range of domains, including image classification, object detection, instance segmentation, generative models, and time-series forecasting. I have hands-on experience with state-of-the-art frameworks and tools such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, YOLO, MediaPipe, and OpenCV, enabling me to build robust and scalable AI solutions.

My work reflects a commitment to excellence, a curiosity for exploring new technologies, and a dedication to delivering impactful results.

Whether it’s building intelligent systems, optimizing models for performance, or contributing to open-source projects, I am driven by a desire to make a meaningful impact through technology.
